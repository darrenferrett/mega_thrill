Thunder Force

Thunder Force is one of the world's largest roller coasters and a one-of-a-kind exclusive to the Mega Thrill Family Fun Theme Park. Come and feel the kind of g-forces only normally experienced by astronauts. Experience the fearsome anticipation before one of our big dips. Thunder Force has more twists and turns than a John Grisham novel and will leave you breathless, wanting to do it all over again.
